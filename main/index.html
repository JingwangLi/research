Jingwang Li

 

About

Since Jun. 2022, I has been an R&D engineer in FABU.AI, where I focus on addressing various optimization problems emerging in autonomous driving. Before that, I was a master student in the School of Artifical Intelligence & Automation of Huazhong University of Science & Technology, where I worked on distributed/decentralized optimization under the supervision of Prof. Housheng Su. Prior to that, I graduated from Huazhong Agricultural University and got a bachelor degree in engineering management.
