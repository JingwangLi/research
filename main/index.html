Research Interests

My research interests mainly focus on theory and applications of decentralized optimization, currently I am interested in developing communication and computation efficient algorithms for a class of decentralized optimization problems with globally coupled constraints. Specifically speaking, consider a networked system composing of n agents, the network topology can be modeled by an undirected or directed graph, then the decentralized optimization problem is defined as

\[ \begin{equation} \label{original_pro} \tag{P1} \begin{aligned} \min_{x_i \in \mathbb{R}^{d_i}} \ & \sum_{i=1}^n f_i(x_i) \\ \text{s.t.} \ & \sum_{i=1}^{n}A_ix_i = b, \end{aligned} \end{equation} \]

where \(f_i:\mathbb{R}^{d_i} \rightarrow \mathbb{R}\) and \(A_i \in \mathbb{R}^{p \times d_i}\) are completely private for agent \(i\) and cannot be shared with its neighbors, while \(b \in \mathbb{R}^{p}\) is public for all agents.
